The Best Movies According to Anthony

My top 3 faovreite movies of all-time.

Battle Royale

A Japanese film that pits high school students against each other in a deadly game of survival. It's a thrilling and thought-provoking movie that explores themes of violence, government control, and human nature.

Baby Driver

A stylish movie with a creative soundtrack and story

Scott Pilgrim vs The World

A quirky and fun movie that combines video game elements with a unique love story. It's visually stunning and has a great cast.
